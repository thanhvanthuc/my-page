How this privacy center works

Product-specific details, without unnecessary complexity.

Different products may use different browser capabilities. For that reason, each privacy policy is maintained separately and describes the behavior that is relevant to that specific product.

Separate policies by product

Each extension or application can have its own privacy page with information tailored to its actual functionality.

Clear data-use explanations

Policies explain what information is processed, stored locally, transmitted, or not collected at all.

Direct support contact

Questions about privacy or product behavior can be sent directly to the developer through the contact information provided on the site.
